Johannes Harms

Usability & Web Design

Johannes works in usability engineering
and writes his PhD on web form design.

“We all like well-designed things. For most of us, this extends to easy-to-use software.”

User Experience

One great goal is to create well-designed software that people like. Since 2009, I have been following this passion working as a Usability Engineer with RISE GmbH.

Technology

I like to engage with the latest web technologies. The projects I have been working on allowed me to gain a lot of experience with tools such as js, css, html, jsf, php, version control, and photoshop.

Doctoral thesis

In my PhD thesis at the DECO research group, I explore innovative ways of improving the usability and interactivity of web forms. Through my research, I aim to demonstrate that good design can turn filling web forms into a pleasant experience.

Education

I graduated from my master studies in computer science at Vienna University of Technology and I am now working on my PhD. I also studied theology at Capernwray Bible School, UK, for a year, but eventually chose to become a software designer / usability engineer.
