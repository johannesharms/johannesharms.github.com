Johannes Harms

Project Manger and UI Designer

Johannes works in project management,
usability engineering and UI design.

“ We all like well-designed things. For most of us, this extends to easy-to-use software. ”

About

Project manager, consultant and usability engineer with RISE GmbH since 2009.

PhD in human-computer interaction (HCI) / web form design with the DECO research group at Vienna University of Technology.

Master in Computer Science and Bachelor in Media Informatics, both at Vienna University of Technology.

I also studied theology at Capernwray Bible School and Vienna University but eventually chose to focus on human aspects of computing, as in human-computer interaction and IT project management.

Industrial Experience

Project management, customer coordination, lead of requirements engineering, scrum master.

Design, UI and UX for web and mobile applications. Mockups and visual design.

Experienced in small and large projects - including big applications with well over 100 screens for web and mobile devices.

Teaching

Guest lecturer at Vienna University of Technology with the DECO research group. Lectures, presentations and seminars about user interface design, usability engineering, and web engineering.

(Co-)supervisor of over 30 bachelor and master theses.
