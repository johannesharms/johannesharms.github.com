Johannes works in usability engineering

“We all like well-designed things. For most of us, this extends to easy-to-use software.”

About

Working as Usability Engineer and UI Designer with RISE GmbH since 2009.

I am writing a PhD thesis about web form design at the DECO research group.

Master in Computer Science / Business Informatics at Vienna University of Technology, bachelor in Media Informatics. I also studied theology at Capernwray Bible School and Vienna University but eventually chose to become a software designer and usability engineer.
