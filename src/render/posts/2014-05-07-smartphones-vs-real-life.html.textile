The "Look Up":http://youtu.be/Z7dLU6fk9QY video argues that smartphones and facebook replace real-world relationships and emotions with dull, technical systems. In order to regain our real lives and find true love, the video's author "Gary Turk":http://www.youtube.com/channel/UCfHJe1XCvPckXPcgKh2V-5A urges us to look up and switch those displays and devices off. I perceive some of the video's critique of technology as overly negative and one-sided, but the author does make his point strongly and emotionally.
